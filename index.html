Our Story (in short)

Every romantic tale has a shorter version of a story, and longer one. I figured that I should remind you of how we happened in short, before I go full Tolstoy

We saw each other

We met at Google Student's Club organized Hangouts 2015. Although there was a spark, you proceeded to ask me, "ARE YOU STALKING ME?"

That First Date

I tried to shrug off my laziness by going for an unconventional date. What was going to be a conversation in the library became one on the stone slabs on the sewers because Pongal. You did not love it, but you did love me.

Them Fights

Of course it was rocky at the beginning. There were fights, those breakups, missed kisses, bad skype chats and innumerable tears. Somewhere in there, I fell in love, knowing you stuck with the asshole I was, told me shit, really empathised with me and even followed me around on that one day.

The Chance

Eventually our equation worked out for the better. In hindsight, any other scenario would have broken down at that time. Guess we were a probabilistic dice roll which was meant to be.

Empathy

We are two very different and very difficult people. Sentimental though. Feeling sad on leaving you standing in front of Tech Park. You feeling sad on listening to your friends over me. We both feeling sad after a fight. And those innumerable expressions of ours. You putting your head on my chest, "Holy Water".....This was bound to be a happy and lasting equation.

Happy Two Years

Eventually, small blips aside, we settled down. Completed one year, then one more. And now, even with distance, the love just does not stop. Surprising though, coz you are a ghochu and I am a tharki. But then, I love you like sharks love blood.
